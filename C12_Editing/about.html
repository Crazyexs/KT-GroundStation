Our Mission

SPACE AC — Groundstation is a collaborative project created to receive real-time telemetry from rockets, visualize and analyze the data, and present it through a clean, user-friendly interface.

Open telemetry. Clear insights. Hands-on learning.

SPACE AC Institute of Technology

SPACE AC Institute of Technology is among the first high-school Aerospace Engineering and Technology learning centers in Thailand. We build strong foundations for students interested in space science and engineering through hands-on, project-based, team-oriented learning.

We actively support national and international competitions and operate research teams targeting publications in international venues, collaborating with schools, universities, research institutes, and government departments.
